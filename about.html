About Straya Mobile Welding

A mobile welding team built for site work, repair work and practical fabrication across Melbourne and Victoria.

Welding support where the work happens.

Straya Mobile Welding helps clients avoid delays by bringing welding capability directly to the job. From breakdown repairs to structural steel and custom fabrication, the focus is reliable workmanship, responsive communication and practical site-ready results.

The business services commercial, industrial, civil and transport work, including trucks, buckets, tippers, excavators, drilling rigs, handrails, gates, fencing, pipe work and steel repairs.
